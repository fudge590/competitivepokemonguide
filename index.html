The original game consists mainly of adventuring, catching pokemon and trainer battles. Items are usually hard to get and many good moves have to be taught to pokemon through Technical Machines(TMs) or Hidden Machines(HMs), which are similarly hard to get.

Differences between the original game and Competitive

Competitive pokemon is, well, competitive. However, there are a lot of rules and restrictions for your preferred gamemode when it comes to competitive, which invites trainers to think out of the box and develop strategies essential to winning. These commonly used strategies and pokemon are called "meta". Some have defined meta as an acronym to mean "Most Effective Tactic Available", which helps in understanding the term, although it did not originate as an acronym.
